Step 1: Basic Information

Search and select country, region, city, and optionally district for this opportunity

Step 2: Intent Label

Choose whether you are seeking work/service or offering work/service. This label is visible everywhere and cannot be changed later.

Choose REQUEST if you need something (e.g. hiring, seeking partners). Choose OFFER if you are providing something (e.g. offering services, selling or bartering resources).

Step 3: Scope & Capabilities

Add required or offered services/skills, sectors, and interests. Used for matching.

Add skills or services; press Enter or comma to add a tag.

Step 4: Category + Sub-Model

Select a category, then choose the sub-model that best fits this opportunity. This defines proposal structure, contract type, and execution style.

Model-Specific Details

Complete the fields below for the selected collaboration type.

Step 6: Review & Publish

Review your opportunity and choose to save as draft or publish.

Draft: Saved but not visible to others. You can edit and publish later.
Published: Visible to all users; matching runs automatically; proposals allowed.

Once published, your opportunity will follow the unified lifecycle: Published → In Negotiation (when proposals arrive) → Contracted → In Execution → Completed → Closed.
